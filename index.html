About the Workshop

Hosted by IGGCAS and co-organized with the Tethyan Dynamics System Major Research Program of NSFC and the International Lithosphere Program, this interdisciplinary workshop will be held in Beijing from July 21–25, 2025. It aims to integrate geology, geophysics, geochemistry, and climate science to explore the role of lithospheric processes in Earth’s habitability.
